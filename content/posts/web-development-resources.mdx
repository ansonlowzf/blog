## Image Optimazation

1. <a href="https://squoosh.app/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">
     Squoosh.app
   </a> {` `} - No ads and free web app. You can preview your image changes on live

     Shrinkme.app
   </a> {` `} - No ads and free web app tool to compress and reduce your image file
