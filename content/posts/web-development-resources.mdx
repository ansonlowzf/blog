## Text Editor

I suggest you try out every text editor listed below.
Find the one you like the most and stick to it until it becomes your second nature.
Every text editor environment is different.
Getting used to the environment make you a faster coder.

If you ask me, I would recommend you to choose VS Code.

1. [VS Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/) - Best free text editor by Microsoft.
1. [Atom](https://atom.io/) - A hackable free text editor for the 21st century.
1. [Sublime Text](https://www.sublimetext.com/) - A sophisticated free or paid text editor for code.
1. [WebStorm](https://www.jetbrains.com/webstorm/) - The smartest JavaScript IDE.
